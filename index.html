Director's Note

Dear Learner,

GIET University is a continuation of the prestigious Gandhi Institute of Engineering and Technology (GIET), which was founded in 1997. Over the years, we have grown, evolved, and expanded our horizons to offer a diverse range of academic programs. Our commitment to quality education and holistic development has made us a trusted name in the field of education.

We invite you to explore our website and learn more about the exceptional opportunities and experiences that await you at GIET University, Gunupur. Whether you are a prospective student, a parent, or a partner institution, we look forward to welcoming you to our community and helping you realize your dreams. Thank you for considering GIET University as your destination for higher education. If you have any questions or need more information, please feel free to contact us. We are here to assist you in your academic journey. Welcome to GIET University, where excellence and innovation meet to shape a brighter future!
