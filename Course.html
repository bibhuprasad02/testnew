GIET University, Gunupur, offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Please note that the specific programs and courses may vary, and it's advisable to visit the official website or contact the university directly for the most up-to-date information. Here is a general overview of the types of programs typically offered: Undergraduate Programs: B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) - Various specializations in engineering disciplines such as Mechanical, Civil, Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Electrical, and more. B.Sc (Bachelor of Science) - Programs in fields like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and others. BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) - For students interested in business and management studies. BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) - Focusing on computer science and application development. B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) - Programs in commerce and related fields. BA (Bachelor of Arts) - Courses in subjects like English, Economics, Political Science, and more. Postgraduate Programs: M.Tech (Master of Technology) - Specialized master's programs in various engineering disciplines. M.Sc (Master of Science) - Advanced studies in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and more. MBA (Master of Business Administration) - Advanced business and management studies. MCA (Master of Computer Applications) - Advanced computer science and application development. M.Com (Master of Commerce) - Advanced programs in commerce and related fields. MA (Master of Arts) - Postgraduate studies in subjects like English, Economics, Political Science, and more. Doctoral and Research Programs: Ph.D. (Doctor of Philosophy) - Research-oriented doctoral programs in various disciplines. M.Phil (Master of Philosophy) - Research-intensive master's programs. These are some of the common programs offered at GIET University, but there may be additional courses and specializations available based on the specific academic departments and faculty expertise. For detailed and up-to-date information on courses, admission criteria, and application procedures, it's recommended to visit the official website or contact the university's admissions office.
